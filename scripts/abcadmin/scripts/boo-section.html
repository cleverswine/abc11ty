About AuntieBooCrafts

Sales 286
On Etsy since 2022

Crafting brings immense joy to my life. I sincerely hope that those who encounter my creatiions also experience that same sense of joy.

One of my earliest memories is a moment in Kindergarten when I painted a self-portrait. To my sheer delight, my artwork earned a spot in a children's artist display at our local museum. The museum staff titled my creation "Happiness," a fitting description as I distinctly remember the joy I felt while crafting it. And, that painting is currently hanging in my home to remind me of that feeling.

Reflecting on my life's journey and the inception of my Etsy shop amid a full-time job, I recognize a consistent thread - my enduring passion for crafting and creating. Throughout the years, whether in high school or college, I found solace in cross-stitching, delved into the world of knitting, and immersed myself in the art of Scrapbooking. Though my creative pursuits evolved, one constant remainedI was always making something.

A brief stint in cookie decorating added a sweet note to my creative endeavors, albeit with consequences for my waistline. Recognizing the need for a change, I redirected my creativity toward custom tumblers, pens, keychains, and badge reels. In 2022, fueled by a leap of faith, I decided to share these creations with a broader audience on Etsy.

The essence of my creative journey lies in the intrinsic joy that crafting brings to my life. Whether it's for myself, my family, or my friends, the act of creation is a perpetual source of happiness.

If you've taken a moment to explore my Etsy shop and read these words, I sincerely hope that something within has sparked a little happiness in you.

Warm regards,

Auntie Boo
